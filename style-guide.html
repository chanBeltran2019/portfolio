MyFlorida: Style Guide

The Task

For those senior project, me and my team were tasked with choosing a website or application to make better and revamp. Me and my team chose the MyFlorida website to redeisgn and redevelop. I took the initiative to be the designer of the team and went ahead a did research.

The Problem

Through analysis, I found the websites major problem was a lack of consistent styling. This was due to the fact that all links on the website lead to external pages on a completely different website. In reality, the website only had one page, the home page. Even considering the inconsistent styling, the styling that was present looked outdated and lackluster.

Solution

Through picking a new and more vibrant color pallete, legible fonts, and consistent styling, we improved the design of MyFlorida. Ensuring the layout of MyFloria was consistent was imperative so users can navigate through the site easily. Design accessibility was taken into account when creating the color choices to ensure a pleasant experiences for all users.

Expected Results

One could expect greater website traffic due to the changes of the website. Users will find a more pleasant experience using the website since it is easier to navigate and more pleasant to look at.
